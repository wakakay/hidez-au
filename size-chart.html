HIDEZ EQUINE SIZING & How To Measure

How To Measure

Obtain an accurate height measurement from the ground to the tallest part of the withers while horse is standing squarely.

Using a flexible tape take a total heart girth measurement. This is the total circumference of the chest, in a vertical line just behind the shoulders .

Refer to the chart below for the correct suit sizing. Suits are sold in half- hands with three fits available: Standard, Mid, and Max.
